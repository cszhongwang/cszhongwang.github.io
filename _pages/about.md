My research lies at the intersection of **3D vision** and **SLAM**, with the ultimate goal of advancing **Spatial Intelligence**.

## 🎓 Prospective Students

I am looking for highly motivated **full-time Master's students** to join my group. 

*   **Training Goal:** My mentoring objective is to equip students with solid scientific research skills and help them publish high-quality papers in top-tier international journals and conferences.
*   **Requirements:** Ideal candidates should have a background in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Communications, Mathematics, or related fields. A strong academic record, good English reading and writing proficiency, a genuine passion for research, and a pragmatic, hard-working attitude are required.
*   **Contact:** If you are interested, please feel free to contact me via email with your CV. We can start our collaboration early through your undergraduate graduation project (FYP).
